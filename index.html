01 About Me

I'm a game developer and software engineer with over a decade of experience building interactive experiences across VR, mobile, and large-scale installations. From architecting multiplayer systems and game servers to crafting gameplay mechanics and developer tools, I thrive on solving complex problems across the full stack.

Most recently, I've been leading multiplayer engineering for a VR live-service title at Owlchemy Labs, and before that I built core systems for Hello Kitty Island Adventure from prototype through launch at Sunblink.
